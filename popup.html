Covid Fact Checker

This extension was created to check and verify COVID related facts found online.

To use this extension you must highlight a word or fact. Once highlighted, right click on the highlighted section and click "Fact Check This". A popup will show up with the highlighted fact being fact checked. Articles will appear with more info about the highlighted topic.
